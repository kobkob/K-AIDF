# Governance

This document defines the canonical governance layer for KAIDF.

## Governance Intent

AI use must be governed explicitly through roles, limits, review, and accountability.

## Suggested Structure

- AI Governance Committee
- AI Governance Lead
- Data and Privacy Lead
- Process owners for each AI-supported workflow
- Periodic internal audit function

## Expected Responsibilities

- approve policies and revisions
- classify risks
- define automation limits
- supervise incidents and audits
- approve training paths
- track institutional maturity

## Governance Rule

AI systems may support decisions and operations, but responsibility for their use and outcomes remains human and institutional.
