# SEO Best Practices

Use this document to adapt the canonical K-AIDF best-practices guidance to search-focused work.

## Focus Areas

- define target audiences, intent clusters, and search journeys
- document information architecture and internal linking assumptions
- validate content quality against relevance, originality, and clarity
- review structured data, crawlability, and discoverability constraints
- keep human review over ranking-risk decisions and content changes

## Operational Checks

- record target queries and the reason they matter
- capture content constraints, including legal and editorial limits
- verify sources, claims, and update cadence
- define quality gates before publication or large-scale content updates
