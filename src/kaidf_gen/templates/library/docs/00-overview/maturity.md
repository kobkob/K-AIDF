# Maturity

This document defines the canonical maturity reference for KAIDF adoption.

## KAIDF Maturity Levels

### Level 1 — Experimental

Use is informal, unstructured, and lacks governance.

### Level 2 — Conscious

The institution recognizes AI use and begins defining guidance.

### Level 3 — Structured

Policies, supervision, and human validation are formalized.

### Level 4 — Integrated

AI is integrated into processes with governance, risk evaluation, and audit.

### Level 5 — Transformative

Responsible AI becomes part of institutional culture and continuous improvement.

## Evaluation Dimensions

- Governance
- AI Use
- Responsibility and Ethics
- Quality and Validation
- Data Protection
- Training
