# Research Best Practices

Use this document to adapt the canonical K-AIDF best-practices guidance to research and analysis workflows.

## Focus Areas

- define research questions, scope, and evidence standards early
- distinguish exploratory outputs from validated conclusions
- document source quality, uncertainty, and known gaps
- require human review before important synthesis or recommendations
- preserve traceability from claims back to supporting evidence

## Operational Checks

- record methodology, assumptions, and exclusion criteria
- separate raw notes from approved findings
- review privacy, ethics, and confidentiality constraints
- define when revalidation is required as inputs change
