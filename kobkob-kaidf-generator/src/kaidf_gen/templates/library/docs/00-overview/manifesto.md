# KAIDF Manifesto

The Knowledge and Artificial Intelligence Development Framework exists to support the ethical, responsible, transparent, and human-centered use of artificial intelligence.

KAIDF treats AI as an amplifier of human capability, not as a substitute for human judgment, institutional responsibility, or ethical accountability.

## Core Commitments

- AI must serve people.
- Responsibility for AI-assisted outcomes remains human.
- Ethics is foundational, not optional.
- Transparency is required wherever trust depends on understanding.
- Automation must operate inside defined limits.
- Human validation is mandatory for relevant outputs.
- Quality comes before speed.
- Data must be protected and respected.
- Continuous learning is necessary for responsible AI use.
- The framework must improve continuously through practice and review.

## Intent

This manifesto anchors the doctrine of KAIDF and should be read together with the canonical principles, best practices, governance, maturity, and implementation documents.
